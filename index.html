About

I spent the last two years evolving in my learning and knowledge, working at a startup called, Tall Chair. Starting as a Technical Artist, moving into an Engineer role working on the Active Reader tool and finishing up as their in house UX Designer & Programmer.

I studied at the Art Institute of California - San Francisco, where I graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Game Art & Design. I currently live in the San Francisco Bay Area, CA.

I am not ready to stop learning. I want my next position to be somewhere that I can grow. Let's learn together!
