I am a generator

After my UX Design course at General Assembly I was told by the industry professional, Luca Candela, that I am a 'generator' and he was a 'specifier'. What this means is that I am some one that pumps out ideas and content and he is the kind of person that selects the idea and helps bring it into focus. We each need each other to get the project together.
